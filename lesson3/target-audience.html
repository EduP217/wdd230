Target Audience and Scenarios

Our target audience is all those local entrepreneurs who live in the city near Leoncio Prado. Enterprises need an entity that allows them to enter a broader market network. That includes those who still need to formalize their business.

Scenarios

  1. You will have full access to the business directory composed by items, sub-items, main contacts and activity abroad. You will be able to geolocate an associated company and at the same time the location of other nearby companies.
  2. Increase your supplier network, improve prices and quality of your supplies, improve your purchasing option.
  3. You will find online the purchasing processes and tenders demanded by the state, as well as advice from specialists.
  4. You will have access to the commercial intelligence platform and the list of demands and offers from abroad, which will allow you to increase your business opportunities abroad.
  5. You will find information on the offer of companies for sale, as well as those interested in buying companies.
