Site Name

LPCC - Leoncio Prado Commerce Chamber

Site Purpose

LPCC assumes the commitment to provide quality business services and business opportunities aimed at satisfying the needs and expectations of our associates.
Through our service, you can receive:

  • Legal guidance on business matters such as tax, labor, commercial, corporate, public contracting, consumer protection, intellectual property, etc., for which we have a staff of top-level lawyers dedicated to the attention of our associates.
  • A business training center responsible for the development and elaboration of business management academic programs that allow preparing and training professionals who face a competitive world today.
  • The Franchise Development Center provides consulting services for those companies that have the potential to franchise their business and thus grow exponentially.
  • We have various virtual platforms, each and every one of them with the aim that you can expand your contacts and be able to help in the promotion of your products and/or services.
